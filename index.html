第一條：就讀班級與班級名稱 / Article 1: Class and Enrollment：
1. 本契約就讀班級名稱依甲方於前項核定選擇之選讀班別為準。本課程為一般系統化正規美語進度班，非屬保證班（無特定保證內容、無保證考取或保證期間等承諾）。
1. The enrolled class name under this contract shall be based on the dynamic course plan checked by Party A. This course is a standard systematic progression class and is NOT a "guaranteed success" or "warranty" program (No specific guarantee contents, guaranteed pass, or warranty period commitments shall apply).
第二條：課程科目、內容與授課時數 / Article 2: Course Subjects, Content, and Weekly Hours：
1. 課程科目內容：Let's Go 兒童美語/Advance青少年進階美語（涵蓋 Phonics 自然發音進階、生活情境對話、聽說讀寫、英檢練習訓練）[cite: 1]。
2. 每週授課時數：時數、班別與時間依據所選班級對應之課表辦理[cite: 1]。
3. 班級編制：採精緻小班制教學，每班實際點名上課人數上限嚴格遵守政府法令規範，以確保教學品質。
1. Course Subject Content: Let's Go Children's English / Advance Junior English (including advanced Phonics, situational dialogues, listening, speaking, reading, writing, and intensive test preparation training)[cite: 1].
2. Weekly Hours: The weekly schedule and class hours shall correspond strictly to the official class timetable designated for the selected course[cite: 1].
3. Class Size: Exquisite small-class system. The number of students per class shall strictly comply with government regulatory limitations to ensure teaching quality.
第三條：修業期間、開課日與插班機制 / Article 3: Course Period, Start Date, and Acknowledgment：
1. 修業期間自民國115年09月起至課程結束止。實際正式開課日為民國115年08月31日，前項期間或開課日之認定，以甲乙雙方約定為準.
2. 插班認定規範：課程開始後始報名插班者，應以報名後最近一次上課日作為開課日（未填列時以甲方實際第一次上課日為開課日），並以該課程剩餘期間及未上課時數作為總課程時數比例計費.
1. The course period starts from September 2026 (Minguo 115) until the end of the course. The official start date is August 31, 2026. The recognition of the aforementioned course period or class start date shall be based on the mutual agreement specified in this contract.
2. Later Enrollment Clause: For students registering after the course has commenced, the next immediate class date after registration shall be recognized as the start date (if left blank, the student's actual first day of class shall be the start date), and the remaining period of the course shall constitute the total course hours for proportional fee calculations.
第四條：收費項目、金額及不當收費之禁止 / Article 4: Fees and Payment Methods：
本課程費用已依據優惠方案進行原價調整，未明列之項目，乙方不得收取[cite: 1]。收取費用應開立正式收據[cite: 1]。
The tuition fees have been adjusted to the standard configuration based on the promotional discount plan. Party B shall not collect any fee items that are not explicitly specified in this contract. Official receipts must be issued for all fees collected[cite: 1].
第五條：桃園市法定退費標準 / Article 5: Refund Policy and Regulations (Taoyuan City Standard)：
依據「桃園市短期補習班退費管理自治條例」規定，學員因個人因素離班者，補習班應於要求退費之日起七日內辦理退費，標準如下：
(1) 實際開課日前第30日以前要求退費者：應退還當期開班依約繳納費用之總額。
(2) 實際開課日前第1日至第29日要求退費者：應退還當期開班依約繳納費用總額之百分之九十。但所收取金額以新臺幣一千元為限，超過部分仍應退還。
(3) 實際開課日起5日內要求退費者：應退還當期開班依約繳納費用總額之百分之八十。
(4) 實際開課日起之第六日，至全期或總課程時數之三分之一期間內要求退費者：應退還當期開班依約繳納費用總額之百分之五十。
(5) 已逾全期或總課程時數之三分之一者：所收取之當期費用，得全數不予退還。
(6) 代辦之教材費若已購置且已發放予甲方者，不予退還，教材歸甲方所有。
In accordance with the Taoyuan City Short-Term Cram School Refund Regulations, if a student leaves due to personal factors, refunds shall be processed within 7 days based on the following standards:
(1) Requested 30 days or more before the class start date: Full 100% refund of total fees paid.
(2) Requested within 1 to 29 days before the start date: 90% refund of the total fees paid (the amount retained by the school is capped at NT$1,000, and any excess must be refunded).
(3) Requested within 5 days from the start date: 80% refund of the total fees paid.
(4) Requested from the 6th day until 1/3 of the total course term: 50% refund of the total fees paid.
(5) Requested after 1/3 of the total course term: Total fees collected may be entirely non-refundable.
(6) If purchased materials or textbooks have already been issued to the student, they are non-refundable and belong to the student.
第六條：請假規範與無故缺課處理 / Article 6: Absence, Rescheduling, and Make-up機制：
1. 學生若因病、事假無法出席課程，家長應於上課前兩小時透過官方 LINE 或電話完成請假程序。
2. 完成請假者，本班將提供該導師安排合適時間進行一對一/小組補課、輔導。
3. 未依規定請假而無故缺課者，視同放棄該堂課程權益，本班不另行安排個人補課，亦不予扣除學費或退費。
1. If a student cannot attend due to sickness or personal matters, parents must request leave via official LINE or phone at least 2 hours prior to the class.
2. For approved leaves, the school will offer 1-on-1 or small-group make-up classes and tutoring arranged by the instructor at a suitable time.
3. Unexcused absences without proper notification constitute an automatic waiver of class rights; no individual make-up classes will be arranged, and no tuition deduction or refund will be provided.
第七條：可歸責乙方之調課補課保障 / Article 7: Rescheduling and Class Fee Refund Protection：
因可歸責於乙方之事由（如教學人員請假等）調動上課時間，致甲方無法配合而缺課者，乙方應予以補課、提供課堂錄影，或經比例計算後返還該節課程費用。
If a class schedule is adjusted or rescheduled due to reasons attributable to Party B (such as instructor leave), resulting in Party A's inability to attend, Party B shall provide make-up classes, offer lesson recordings, or refund the specific class fee proportionally based on calculations.
第八條：專業師資與場所安全保障限制 / Article 8: Rights, Obligations, and Faculty Qualifications：
1. 乙方應確保教學場所之公共安全、消防設施及公共意外責任險符合政府法令規範。
2. 乙方所聘任之任課教師（如 Bobby (林仲齊)、Joy (林婷玉) 等教學人員）皆應具備合格教學資格，並落實無犯罪紀錄之實名防查登記。無正當事由不得任意更換教學人員，更換時人數不得超過三分之一，且須以相同專業資格者為限。
3. 變更合約終止：乙方無正當事由變更原訂課程、科目、時數、上課地點或更換教學人員超過三分之一人數者，甲方得終止契約，乙方應依甲方已繳金額費用加計30%退還甲方。
1. Party B shall ensure that public safety, fire facilities, and public liability insurance of the teaching venue comply with government laws and regulations.
2. Instructors hired by Party B (such as Teacher Bobby (林仲齊), Teacher Joy (林婷玉)) must possess qualified teaching credentials and pass real-name criminal background checks. Instructors shall not be replaced without justification; replacement is strictly capped at 1/3 and restricted to equivalent professional qualifications.
3. Termination due to Alterations: If Party B alters the original course, subject, hours, location, or replaces instructors by more than 1/3 without cause, Party A may terminate the contract, and Party B must refund the fees paid plus an extra 30% penalty.
第九條：違規行為處置與班級秩序終止 / Article 9: Student Conduct and Contract Termination：
甲方於修業期間應遵守課堂秩序，不得有干擾他人學習、破壞公物之行為。若有嚴重危害班級安全、竊奪或違法行為且屢勸不聽者，乙方得終止本契約，並依第七條（即本定型化契約第五條）退費手續辦理。
Party A must strictly maintain classroom order during the course period and shall not disrupt others' learning or vandalize public property. For major disruptions endangering safety, thefts, or illegal acts where repeated warnings fail, Party B may terminate this contract and issue refunds according to the refund policy.
第十條：肖像權合理授權與個資隱私保護 / Article 10: Portrait Rights and Personal Data Privacy Protection：
1. 甲方同意乙方於課堂教學、節慶活動 or 成果發表中進行拍照及錄影，並授權乙方得將其影像合理使用於補習班內部學習成果展示、官方網站或招生推廣文宣，且不損及學生名譽。
2. 未經甲方書面同意，乙方不得擅自將甲方個人資料提供給第三人或作不當利用。乙方應严格遵守《個人資料保護法》之規定，僅用於教學行政與聯絡。
1. Party A agrees that Party B may take photos and videos during classes, holiday events, or achievement presentations, and authorizes Party B to reasonably use the images for internal displays, the official website, or promotional marketing materials, provided it doesn't harm the student's reputation.
2. Without explicit written consent, Party B shall not provide personal data of Party A to third parties or utilize it improperly. Party B must strictly handle all data in full compliance with the Personal Data Protection Act solely for tutoring administration and communication.

1. In case of doubts regarding contract clauses, interpretations shall lean in favor of Party A (the consumer) based on Article 11, Paragraph 2 of the Consumer Protection Act.
