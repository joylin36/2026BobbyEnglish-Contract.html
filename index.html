第一條：修業班別與班級性質 / Article 1: Course and Program Enrollment Type：
1. 本契約核定就讀班級為：Let's Go 1 兒童美語初級班。
2. 本課程屬一般系統化正規美語教學進度班，非屬保證班（補習班未提供任何特定考取、分數擔保、退費退分等特定保證成效承諾）。
1. The approved program under this contract is: Let's Go 1 Children's English Elementary Class.
2. This course is a regular systematic progression program and is NOT a "guaranteed success" or "warranty" program (No promises of passing specific exams or special (effectiveness or outcome) guarantee warranties shall apply).
第二條：修業期間與插班計費規範 / Article 2: Study Period and Pro-Rata Later Enrollment Policy：
1. 本期核定修業期間自民國115年08月31日正式開課起，至該班當期排定課程總堂數（51堂課）結束止。
2. 課程開始後報名插班者，應以實際報名後之最近一次實際上課日作為開課日（未填列時以甲方實際第一次上課日為準），並以該課程當期剩餘期間及未上課時數，依比例計費收取費用。
1. The term of study commences on the official start date of August 31, 2026, until the scheduled conclusion of the entire semester program lessons.
2. For students enrolling after the course has commenced, the immediate next actual class date after formal registration shall be deemed the official start date, and fees shall be calculated pro-rata based on the remaining lessons and period.
第三條：課程科目、內容與授課時數 / Article 3: Course Subjects, Content, and Timetable Hours：
1. 課程科目與核心內容：Let's Go 1 美語基礎教學（涵蓋 26 字母標準拼讀認讀、Phonics 自然發音基礎啟盟、日常情境指令與生活社交對話、高頻率字彙視覺記憶養成）。
2. 每週授課時數與時間：每週授課 3 次，每次 2 小時，每週共計 6 小時（每週一、三、五 下午 1:30 - 3:30 p.m.）。
1. Course Subjects and Core Content: Let's Go 1 Foundational English (including standard alphabet writing/recognition, Phonics enlightenment, classroom directions, situational daily dialogues, and visual vocabulary training).
2. Weekly Hours: 3 times a week, 2 hours per lesson, totaling 6 hours per week (Every Mon, Wed, Fri from 1:30 PM to 3:30 PM).
第四條：班級編制與實際上課人數上限 / Article 4: Class Size and Legal Capacity Limitations：
1. 本班採取精緻小班制教學，實際點名上課人數上限嚴格遵守 government 法令規範限制（本班招生人數上限為10人），以確保教學品質與場所公共安全。
1. Exquisite small-class system. The number of students per class shall strictly comply with government regulatory limitations and is strictly capped at 10 students maximum to ensure teaching quality and venue safety.
第五條：收費項目、金額與禁止不當收費 / Article 5: Fee Items, Grand Total, and Receipt Requirements：
1. 本期核定收費費用包含基本學費、教材講義費及當期學雜費，當期實際應繳總金額以本合約第一項動態計算扣除優惠減免後之實際金額為準（目前核定原價總額為新台幣 35,948 元）。
2. 未明列於契約內之項目，乙方不得收取。所有收取費用乙方應依法開立正式核定收據憑證交予甲方。
1. The tuition and fees consist of Base Tuition, Textbooks/Materials, and Fees, and the total payment shall be based on the actual Grand Total dynamically computed under Section 1 after discount deductions.
2. Party B shall not collect any fee items that are not explicitly specified herein under any pretext. Official receipts must be issued for all fees collected.
第六條：桃園市法定退費標準 / Article 6: Refund Policy and Regulations (Taoyuan City Standard)：
依據「桃園市短期補習班退費管理自治條例」規範，學員因故離班退費標準條例嚴格執行如下（補習班應於家長要求退費之日起七日內辦理完畢）：
(1) 實際開課日前第30日以前要求退費者：退還當期依約繳納費用之總額（100%全退）。
(2) 實際開課日前第1日至第29日要求退費者：退還繳納費用總額之百分之九十（90%）。但所扣除金額以新臺幣一千元為限，超過部分仍應退還。
(3) 實際開課日起5日內要求退費者：退還依約繳納費用總額之百分之八到（80%）。
(4) 實際開課日起第六日，至全期或總課程時數之三分之一期間內要求退費者：退還依約繳納費用總額之百分之五十（50%）。
(5) 已逾全期或總課程時數之三分之一者：所收取之當期費用得全數不予退還。
(6) 代辦之教材費若已購置並已發放予甲方者，該項目不予退還，教材歸甲方所有。
In accordance with the Taoyuan City Cram School Refund Regulations, refunds shall be processed within 7 days based on the following:
(1) Requested 30 days or more before start date: Full 100% refund of total fees paid.
(2) Requested within 1 to 29 days before start date: 90% refund of total fees paid (the amount retained by school is capped at NT$1,000, excess must be refunded).
(3) Requested within 5 days from the start date: 80% refund of total fees paid.
(4) Requested from the 6th day until 1/3 of total course term: 50% refund of total fees paid.
(5) Requested after 1/3 of total course term: Fees collected may be entirely non-refundable.
(6) Issued materials or textbooks are non-refundable and belong to the student.
第七條：學員請假規範與導師編制補課機制 / Article 7: Absence, Leave Notification, and Make-up Tutoring：
1. 學生若因病、因事假無法出席，監護人應於上課前兩小時透過官方 LINE 帳號或專線完成請假登記。
2. 凡完成合規請假者，本班將指派該班導師安排合適時間，提供一對一 or 小組專業輔導、補課 or 提供課堂精華錄影。
3. 未依規定請假無故缺課者，視同自動放棄該堂課程權益，本班不另行補課，亦不予扣除學費 or 退費。
1. If a student cannot attend due to sickness or personal matters, parents must request leave via official LINE or phone at least 2 hours prior to class.
2. For approved leaves, the school will arrange 1-on-1 or small-group instructor-led make-up classes or supplementary tutoring.
3. Unexcused absences without proper notification constitute a waiver of lesson rights; no make-up sessions or tuition deductions shall apply.
第八條：可歸責乙方之調課補課與退費保障 / Article 8: Alterations Attributable to Party B and Penalty Refund：
1. 因可歸責於乙方之原因（如教師請假等）調動上課時間，致甲方無法配合而缺課者，乙方應無條件予以補課、提供課堂影音，或按比例計算返還該節課程費用。
2. 乙方無正當事由變更原訂課程、科目、時數、上課地點 or 更換教學人員超過三分之一人數者，甲方得宣告終止契約，乙方應依甲方已繳金額費用加計30%作為違約金損害賠償退還甲方。
1. If a class schedule is adjusted due to reasons attributable to Party B (such as instructor leave), resulting in Party A's inability to attend, Party B shall provide alternative make-up sessions or refund the specific lesson fee proportionally.
2. If Party B alters the original course, subject, hours, or replaces instructors by more than 1/3 without cause, Party A may terminate the contract, and Party B must refund all fees paid plus an extra 30% penalty.
第九條：肖像權合理使用授權與個資隱私安全保護 / Article 9: Portrait Rights and Personal Data Privacy Protection：
1. 甲方同意並授權乙方得於常態教學、成果發表或特殊節慶活動中進行拍照與錄影，並得將學員影像合理、不損及名譽地使用於補習班內部學習成果展示、官方網站或招生行銷文宣。
2. 乙方應營格遵守《個人資料保護法》規範，甲方所填報之所有個資僅得用於補習班內部教學行政與安全緊急聯絡，未經書面允許，絕不得洩漏給任何第三人。
1. Party A authorizes Party B to take photos and videos during regular classes or holiday events, and reasonably use the images for internal achievement displays or official website promotional marketing, provided it doesn't harm the student's reputation.
2. Party B must strictly handle all personal data in full compliance with the Personal Data Protection Act solely for academic administration, safety, and urgent communication. Selling or disclosing data to third parties is strictly prohibited.

學員於修業期間應共同維護課堂秩序與安全。若有嚴重干擾他人學習、惡意破壞公共設施、侵害違法行為且屢勸不聽者，為維護全班學員安全防線，乙方得單方面終止本契約，並依第六條退費條款辦理剩餘款項退費手續。
Students must strictly maintain classroom order and safety.
